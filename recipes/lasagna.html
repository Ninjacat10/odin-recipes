Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for lasagna. It includes a lot of ingredients, but not many instructions. It is a classic Italian dish that is easy to make and serves a lot of people.

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish that typically includes meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It is baked in the oven until bubbly and golden brown.

Enjoy this hearty and comforting meal with your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Cheese
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles

Instructions

  1. Cook the meat with onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, and spices. Simmer.
  3. Layer meat sauce, cheese, and noodles in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  6. Let it cool before serving.
  7. Slice and serve.
