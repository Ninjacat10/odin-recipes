Salad

Description

This is a recipe for salad. It is healthy.

Salad is a versatile dish that can be made with various fresh vegetables and toppings. It is a great way to include more greens in your diet and can be customized to your taste.

Enjoy this refreshing salad as a side dish or a light meal!

  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

  • Garlic
  • Red wine vinegar

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce, tomato, and cucumber.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chopped vegetables and toss to combine.
  4. Optional: Add sliced onions or other toppings as desired.
  5. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator before serving.
