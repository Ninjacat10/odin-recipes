Pancakes

Description

This is a recipe for pancakes. It is a yummy food.

Pancakes are a breakfast favorite that can be enjoyed with various toppings like syrup, fruits, or whipped cream. They are quick to make and perfect for a weekend brunch.

Enjoy these fluffy pancakes with your favorite toppings!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until combined.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and grease it.
  4. Pour batter onto the skillet and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve with syrup or toppings of your choice.
  7. Repeat for remaining pancakes.
